About Us

In the years before COVID-19 hybrid working was just becoming a thing. A few of us worked from home one or two days a week, but Teams meetings were awkward affairs and we used that time mostly to focus on our own work.

Fast forward to 2020 and suddenly we were all doing it. Everybody was working from home because we had to, and Teams meetings started to work as we all just got used to them.

Now many of us have got the taste for hybrid working and never looked back. We don't miss the commute at all. And it makes things a whole lot easier if you're one of two working parents.

What has that meant for the office?

Company offices sized for dozens have a handful of people most days. Or offices mostly empty all week except for the company office day, when we all get together. The only thing that hasn't changed is the rent.

We had the brain wave that many companies only need that one office day a week. Everybody wants offices that are their space alone, not shared with a dozen other companies. But CBD offices are expensive, and nobody wants to pay for office space they don't use.

Now you can have it both ways!
